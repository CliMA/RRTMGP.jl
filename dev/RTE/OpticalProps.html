    RRTMGP.OpticalProps.OpticalPropsBase — Type.
    OpticalPropsBase{FT,I} <: AbstractOpticalProps{FT,I}

    Base class for optical properties. Describes the spectral discretization including the wavenumber limits of each band (spectral region) and the mapping between g-points and bands.

    • (begin g-point, end g-point) = band2gpt(2,band)
    • band = gpt2band(g-point)
    • (upper and lower wavenumber by band) = bandlimswvn(2,band)
